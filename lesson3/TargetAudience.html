Audience

My main Audience is local business owners in New York City or someone looking to start their own business. However our Audience could be people moving from a different state to New York and wants to have a business in New York.

Britney is a owner of clothing business in Yonkers New York. She is earning great revenue that now she wants to start a newn business of catering in Broklyn New York.

Carlos is a owner of a meat factory at Mesa, Arizona. He wish to move to the coast and his prefrence is New York since his family live here. He is dealing with NYC Chambers of Commerce to help him find the way to move his business from Arizona to New York.
